Compostable bag

White or light green semi-transparent bags with grid pattern. Often has a mily aspect. If you see the typical grid pattern, please tag as compostable bag (even if you see a small part of it)

Examples


Torn compostable bag

Torn parts of degraded compostable bags. Looks like thin, elongated strips of semi-translucent or slightly milky material.
You might not see the grid because the material is decomposed.
